Maya Man (she/her) is an artist, programmer, and dancer based in New York City.

Currently, she's building experiments at the Google Creative Lab after completing the one year Fiver residency program. She holds a B.A. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Media Studies with a focus in digital production from Pomona College.

Maya spends most of her time creating work for the world wide web, dancing in studios and public spaces, and maintaining her love/hate relationship with the Internet (mostly love though). She was recently named one of Dance Magazine's "25 to Watch" in 2020.

Say hello@mayaontheinter.net or find her on Instagram, Twitter, Github, and Are.na.
