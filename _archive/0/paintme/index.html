Paint me in pixels so I can dance forever

This series exhibits the output of a computer program written to continually layer a dancer's form on screen, creating a unique portrait of their past movement. While any motion performed in ~real life~ lasts only a moment, by rendering myself in pixels I am able to allow my movement to linger on screen indefinitely.

Shown at CADAF Miami for Art Basel and at Times Square and Lightbox in New York City.
