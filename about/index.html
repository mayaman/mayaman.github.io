Maya Man
~ info ~

Maya Man is a New York City based artist, technologist, and dancer who creates work with code.

Currently, she's building experiments at the Google Creative Lab after completing the fiver residency program. Maya graduated cum laude from Pomona College with a double major in Computer Science and Media Studies with a focus in digital production.

She spends most of her time creating weird web things, dancing in studios and public spaces, and maintaining her love/hate relationship with the internet (mostly love though). She was recently named one of Dance Magazine's "25 to Watch" in 2020.

Say hi@mayaontheinter.net or find her on instagram, twitter, and github.
